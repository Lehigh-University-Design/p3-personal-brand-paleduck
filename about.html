About Mario

Mario, the founder and co-owner of Super Mario Bros Plumbing, is a multi-talented man with a love for helping people and adventure. During times of peace in the Mushroom Kingdom, with the help of his brother, Luigi, he makes it a mission to protect the plumbing of people in need throughout New York.
